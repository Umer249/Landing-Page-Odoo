Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

You don’t pay more — that’s the point of fixed-scope pricing. During discovery, we define exactly what’s being delivered: modules, users, warehouses, reports, integrations, and data migration volume. Everything inside that scope is covered, regardless of how many hours it actually takes us. If it runs long, that’s on us, not you.

If you decide mid-project to add something outside the original scope — say, a new module, an extra integration, or a custom workflow we didn’t plan for — we send you a written change order with a fixed add-on price before any work starts. Nothing happens without your sign-off.

Data migration is included in every tier. We handle your customer master, supplier master, product catalog with SKUs, open invoices (AR/AP), and opening stock balances as standard. Tier limits apply by volume; anything beyond that is scoped during discovery so you know the cost upfront.

The process: we give you templated extract sheets, your team (or ours, if you prefer) fills them in from your existing system, we validate the data, run a dry-run migration to staging for you to review, then migrate to production the weekend before go-live. Nothing goes live until you’ve tested and signed off.

By default we deploy on Odoo Online, which runs on Google Cloud infrastructure with automated daily backups, SSL encryption, and 99.9% uptime. Your data is yours — you can download a full backup at any time, and if you ever leave Odoo, you leave with your complete database.

If you have specific requirements around UAE data residency, a private hosting environment, or you want full server access for custom development, we deploy on Odoo.sh or a dedicated cloud instance instead — we’ll walk through the options in the discovery call based on your compliance needs.

Most projects start with a discovery call, a short process review, and a fixed-scope proposal. Once the scope is signed, we can usually begin within a week and keep the rollout focused on the four-week go-live plan.
